<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Nebuliser</li><li>Ultrasonic Nebuliser</li><li>Mesh Nebuliser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nebulizer market encompasses three main types: pneumatic, ultrasonic, and mesh nebulizers. Pneumatic nebulizers utilize compressed air to convert liquid medication into mist, making them widely used and cost-effective. Ultrasonic nebulizers employ high-frequency vibrations to aerosolize medication, allowing for a quiet operation and fine mist output. Mesh nebulizers use a vibrating mesh to generate aerosol particles, offering portability and efficient medication delivery. Each type serves distinct therapeutic needs in respiratory care, impacting patient compliance and treatment effectiveness.</p></p>
