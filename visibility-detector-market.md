<p><ul><li> Perspective</li><li>Scattering</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The visibility detector market is primarily segmented into two types: perspective and scattering. </p><p>Perspective visibility detectors assess distances based on angular measurements, providing data on visible ranges influenced by atmospheric conditions. Scattering detectors, on the other hand, measure light particles dispersed by atmospheric elements, determining visibility through the analysis of scattering phenomena. Both types play critical roles in enhancing safety in transportation, contributing to environmental monitoring, and supporting various industrial applications by accurately measuring visibility levels.</p></p>
