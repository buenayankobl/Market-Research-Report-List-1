<p><strong>The AR Cloud Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Marker Based</li><li>Marker Less</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR Cloud market can be categorized into two main types: Marker-Based and Marker-Less. Marker-Based AR utilizes physical markers, like QR codes or images, to trigger digital content, creating a seamless interaction between the real and virtual worlds. In contrast, Marker-Less AR does not rely on specific markers; instead, it uses environmental features, GPS, and sensors to anchor digital content within a user’s surroundings, offering a more flexible and immersive experience. Both categories enhance user engagement and interactivity.</p></p>
