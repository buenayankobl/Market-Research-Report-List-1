<p><p>A pulsatile blood pump is a mechanical device designed to replicate the natural rhythm of the heart by delivering blood in discrete pulses, rather than a continuous flow. This technology is commonly used in various medical applications, including cardiopulmonary bypass during heart surgeries and as a treatment for patients with severe heart failure. The pulsatile action is beneficial as it promotes better blood circulation, reduces the risk of thrombus formation, and provides better end-organ perfusion.</p><p>The Pulsatile Blood Pump Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period. Recent trends indicate a growing interest in miniaturization and portable designs, enhancing the usability of these pumps in both hospital and home settings.
