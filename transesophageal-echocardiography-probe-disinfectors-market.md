<p><p>The transesophageal echocardiography (TEE) probe disinfectors market is categorized into two primary types based on disinfection levels: Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD). LLDs/ILDs are suitable for semi-critical devices, effectively reducing microbial load but not eliminating all pathogens. In contrast, HLD involves more rigorous processes to ensure complete pathogen eradication, critical for high-risk procedures. Both categories are essential for maintaining patient safety and preventing infections associated with TEE probes in clinical settings.</p></p>
