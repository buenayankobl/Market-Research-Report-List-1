<p><p>The analog panel meters market is primarily divided into two types: AC analog panel meters and DC analog panel meters. AC analog panel meters measure alternating current and voltage, displaying readings on a scale typically using a moving coil mechanism. They are commonly used in electrical applications where AC power is prevalent. Conversely, DC analog panel meters measure direct current and voltage, serving applications in battery-operated devices and systems. Both types provide visual feedback for monitoring and controlling electrical parameters in various industries.</p></p>
