<p><strong>The Amplifiers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-A/B</li><li>Class-G&H</li><li>Class-D</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The amplifier market consists of several types, each defined by their design and efficiency. Class-A amplifiers provide high linearity but are less efficient, making them suitable for high-fidelity audio. Class-B amplifiers offer improved efficiency for larger outputs but introduce distortion at low power. Class-A/B combines both technologies for balanced performance. Class-G and Class-H amplifiers enhance efficiency by dynamically adjusting voltage supply levels. Class-D amplifiers utilize pulse width modulation for high efficiency and compact design, making them popular in modern audio systems.</p></p>
