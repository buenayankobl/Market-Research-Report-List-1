<p><ul><li>Nerve Conduit</li><li>Nerve Wrap and Cap</li><li>Nerve Graft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The peripheral nerve repair market comprises several key types: nerve conduits, nerve wraps and caps, and nerve grafts. Nerve conduits serve as channels for guiding regenerating nerve fibers across gaps. Nerve wraps and caps provide protective environments for injured nerves, supporting healing. Nerve grafts involve transplanting healthy nerve segments to bridge damaged areas, often harvested from the patient. Together, these solutions facilitate effective nerve repair and regeneration, catering to various severities and types of peripheral nerve injuries.</p></p>
