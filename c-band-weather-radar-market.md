<p><p>The C-Band Weather Radar market is primarily categorized into two types: Single Polarization and Dual Polarization. Single Polarization systems transmit and receive signals in a single orientation, making them effective for basic precipitation detection and intensity assessment. In contrast, Dual Polarization radars emit signals in both horizontal and vertical orientations, enabling enhanced capabilities to distinguish between different types of precipitation, such as rain, snow, and hail. This added functionality supports improved weather forecasting and analysis, leading to better decision-making in meteorology and disaster management.</p></p>
