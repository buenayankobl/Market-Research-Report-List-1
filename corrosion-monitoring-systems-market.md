<p><p>Corrosion monitoring systems are essential for assessing material degradation in various industries. Intrusive corrosion monitoring systems involve techniques like probes and sensors that require physical installation within the infrastructure, providing precise data on corrosion rates. Conversely, non-intrusive corrosion monitoring systems use external methods such as electromagnetic or ultrasonic technologies that do not compromise structural integrity. These systems are crucial for preventive maintenance, enhancing safety, reducing downtime, and prolonging the lifespan of assets in sectors like oil and gas, water treatment, and manufacturing.</p></p>
