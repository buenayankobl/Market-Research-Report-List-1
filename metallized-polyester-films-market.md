<p><p>Metallized polyester films are thin sheets made from polyester that have a metallic layer, usually aluminum, deposited on their surface. Emerging trends such as the adoption of eco-friendly films and innovations in functionality, such as anti-static and UV-resistant properties, are also shaping the market landscape.
