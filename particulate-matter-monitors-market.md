<p><strong>The Particulate Matter Monitors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>TEOM Monitor</li><li>Beta Attenuation Monitor</li><li>Other Monitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Particulate Matter (PM) monitors are crucial for assessing air quality. The TEOM (Tapered Element Oscillating Microbalance) monitor measures PM mass by collecting particles on a filter and using oscillation frequency changes to determine weight. The Beta Attenuation Monitor employs beta radiation to quantify PM by measuring the attenuation caused by particles on a filter. Other monitors include optical particle counters and gravimetric monitors, each using different techniques to detect and quantify particulate matter in the environment.</p></p>
