<p><ul><li>Suspension Type</li><li>Roller Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vacuum plating machine market consists of primarily two types: suspension type and roller type. Suspension type machines utilize a suspended arrangement to evenly coat substrates with thin films, ideal for delicate or irregularly shaped items. In contrast, roller type machines rely on rotating rollers to enable continuous processing of materials, allowing for higher production speeds and efficiency, especially suited for flat or uniform surfaces. Both types are essential in various industries for enhancing surface properties and aesthetics of products.</p></p>
