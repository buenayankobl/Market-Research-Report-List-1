<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Diesel Centrifugal Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Flow Pumps</li><li>Mixed/Radial Flow Pumps</li><li>Peripheral Pumps</li><li>Jet Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diesel centrifugal pump market comprises several types tailored to specific applications. Axial flow pumps efficiently move fluids parallel to the pump's axis, ideal for high flow rates at low pressure. Mixed/radial flow pumps combine axial and radial flow characteristics, suitable for moderate flow and pressure requirements. Peripheral pumps, known for their compact design, are used in low-flow, high-pressure applications. Jet pumps utilize a jet of fluid to create a vacuum, drawing liquid up from a source, useful in lifting groundwater.</p></p>
