<p><p>The on-hand ventilator market is categorized into three main types: electronic, pneumatic, and electro-pneumatic ventilators. </p><p>Electronic ventilators rely on sophisticated software and sensors to deliver precise ventilation support, offering advanced features for patient monitoring and customization. </p><p>Pneumatic ventilators operate using compressed air, making them simpler and often more robust, ideal for emergency situations. </p><p>Electro-pneumatic ventilators combine both technologies, utilizing electronic controls for flexibility while retaining pneumatic action, providing reliability and versatility in diverse medical settings.</p></p>
