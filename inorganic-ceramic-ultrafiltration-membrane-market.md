<p><p>The Inorganic Ceramic Ultrafiltration Membrane market comprises two primary types: tubular membranes and flat-sheet membranes. Tubular membranes feature a cylindrical design that allows for high flow rates and easy cleaning, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, flat-sheet membranes consist of planar sheets arranged in modules, providing larger surface areas for filtration and efficient space utilization. Both types exhibit excellent thermal and chemical resistance, making them ideal for various industries, including water treatment, food processing, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
