<p><p>The local cryotherapy market encompasses three main types: Liquid Nitrogen Therapy, Dry Ice Therapy, and Electric Cryotherapy. Liquid Nitrogen Therapy utilizes extremely cold liquid nitrogen to freeze targeted tissues, offering effective pain relief and skin treatment. Dry Ice Therapy employs solid carbon dioxide to achieve similar cooling effects, often used for localized skin conditions. Electric Cryotherapy, on the other hand, uses electric devices to deliver controlled cold temperatures, providing a non-invasive option for pain management and recovery. Each type serves specific therapeutic needs.</p></p>
