<p><p>The Percutaneous Transluminal Angioplasty (PTA) balloons market features several types tailored for varying vascular interventions. Drug-eluting balloons release medication to prevent restenosis, enhancing long-term efficacy. Normal balloons are standard devices used to dilate narrowed vessels. Scoring balloons incorporate small blades that create controlled incisions in the arterial wall to facilitate expansion. Cutting balloons combine inflation with cutting action to prepare lesions for optimized dilation. Each type addresses specific clinical needs and patient outcomes in vascular therapy.</p></p>
