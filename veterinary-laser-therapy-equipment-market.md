<p><p>Veterinary laser therapy equipment is categorized into Class 3B and Class IV lasers. Class 3B lasers are lower power devices, typically used for superficial treatments, providing localized pain relief and promoting wound healing. In contrast, Class IV lasers offer higher power output, enabling deeper tissue penetration and more effective treatment for various conditions, such as arthritis and soft tissue injuries. Both types enhance healing and reduce inflammation, but their applications and depth of treatment vary based on the laser class.</p></p>
