<p><p>The VIS-SWIR camera market encompasses two primary types: area scan cameras and line scan cameras. Area scan cameras capture an entire image in a single exposure, making them ideal for applications requiring rapid image processing, such as surveillance and machine vision. Line scan cameras, on the other hand, acquire images one line at a time, making them suitable for continuous processes like web inspection or industrial automation. Both types leverage visible and shortwave infrared wavelengths for enhanced imaging versatility and precision.</p></p>
