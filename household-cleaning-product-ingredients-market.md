<p><p>The household cleaning product ingredients market is categorized into several types. Chlorine compounds, often used for their disinfecting properties, include bleach and are effective against bacteria and viruses. Quaternary ammonium compounds, or quats, are popular for their antimicrobial activity and are found in many surface cleaners. Alcohols, such as ethanol and isopropyl alcohol, offer quick-drying sanitizing benefits. Phenolic compounds provide antibacterial effectiveness but can be harsher on surfaces. Other ingredients encompass various surfactants and enzymes that enhance cleaning performance.</p></p>
