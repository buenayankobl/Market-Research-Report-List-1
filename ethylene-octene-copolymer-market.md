<p><p>Ethylene Octene Copolymer (EOC) is categorized into three main market types: Injection Grade, General Grade, and Extrusion Grade. Injection Grade EOC is designed for producing intricate molded parts, offering superior flow and flexibility. General Grade provides a balance of properties for various applications, including films and coatings. Extrusion Grade is specifically formulated for processes like blown or cast film production, emphasizing high melt strength and processability. Each grade targets distinct industrial demands, enhancing versatility in applications.</p></p>
