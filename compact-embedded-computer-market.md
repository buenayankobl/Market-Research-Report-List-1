Fanned embedded computers utilize cooling fans to dissipate heat, making them suitable for high-performance applications where thermal management is crucial. In contrast, fanless embedded computers rely on passive cooling solutions, offering a silent, energy-efficient option ideal for environments sensitive to noise or dust. Both types cater to diverse industries, including industrial automation, transportation, and smart IoT devices, ensuring efficient operation in confined spaces.</p></p>
