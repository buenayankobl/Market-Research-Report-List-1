<p><ul><li>Solenoid Type</li><li>Piezo Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Diesel Common Rail Injection System market primarily comprises two types: solenoid type and piezo type injectors. Solenoid type injectors use electromagnetic solenoids to control fuel injection, offering good performance at a lower cost. Conversely, piezo type injectors utilize piezoelectric crystals for rapid and precise fuel delivery, enhancing efficiency and reducing emissions. Both types play vital roles in meeting stringent emission regulations, with increasing demand for fuel-efficient and environmentally friendly vehicles driving their market growth.</p></p>
