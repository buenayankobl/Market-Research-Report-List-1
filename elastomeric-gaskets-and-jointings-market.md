The market is primarily segmented into two types: rubber and thermoplastic elastomers (TPEs). Rubber gaskets provide excellent flexibility, resilience, and resistance to various chemicals and temperatures, making them suitable for demanding environments. Thermoplastic elastomers offer versatile performance, combining the characteristics of rubber and plastic, enabling easier processing and recycling while maintaining effective sealing properties.</p></p>
