<p><p>The multi-room ceiling speaker market is primarily divided into two types: WiFi and Bluetooth multi-room ceiling speakers. WiFi multi-room ceiling speakers connect to a home network, enabling seamless streaming across multiple rooms with better sound quality and range. They allow for centralized control through mobile apps and can integrate with smart home systems. Conversely, Bluetooth multi-room ceiling speakers offer a more straightforward setup, allowing users to connect devices directly without a network, but may have limited range and functionality compared to WiFi models.</p></p>
