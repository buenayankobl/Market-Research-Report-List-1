<p><p>Pouch films encompass various types of materials tailored for packaging solutions. Aluminum foil offers excellent barrier properties, making it ideal for food and pharmaceutical products. BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film is lightweight and offers clarity, commonly used for snacks and labels. BOPA (Biaxially Oriented Polyamide) film provides strength and moisture resistance, suitable for sensitive contents. BOPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) film boasts high tensile strength and thermal stability. CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film is flexible and seals well, while LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) film offers durability and puncture resistance. Other types may include additional specialized films for niche applications.</p></p>
