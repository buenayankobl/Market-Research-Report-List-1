<p><p>The denture repair material market includes two primary types: powder formats and liquid formats. Powder formats typically consist of a powdered resin or adhesive mixed with a liquid activator to create a strong bond for repairing dentures. These materials often require precise measurements and are popular for their versatility and ease of storage. Liquid formats, on the other hand, come pre-mixed for convenience and immediate application, providing quick fixes for denture fractures or breaks without the need for additional mixing.</p></p>
