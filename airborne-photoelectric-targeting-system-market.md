<p><p>The Airborne Photoelectric Targeting System market encompasses various technologies, primarily multispectral and hyperspectral systems. Multispectral systems capture images at a limited number of wavelengths, focusing on key bands to identify objects and assess environmental conditions. In contrast, hyperspectral systems gather data across a broader spectrum, capturing hundreds of bands, providing detailed information on material composition and structure. Both types serve critical roles in surveillance, reconnaissance, and target acquisition, enhancing military and civilian operational effectiveness with advanced imaging capabilities.</p></p>
