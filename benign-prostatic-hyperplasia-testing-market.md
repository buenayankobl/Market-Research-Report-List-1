Urodynamic tests evaluate bladder function and urine flow, helping to understand the impact of BPH on urinary symptoms. Cystoscopy involves inserting a scope into the bladder for direct visualization, allowing for detailed assessment of the prostate and urinary tract. These tests play vital roles in diagnosis and management of BPH.</p></p>
