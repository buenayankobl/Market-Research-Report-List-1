<p><p>Coagulation analysers are critical in diagnosing clotting disorders, with three main market types: standalone analysers, table-top analysers, and portable analysers. Standalone analysers are large, high-capacity machines suitable for laboratory use, offering comprehensive testing capabilities. Table-top analysers are compact, designed for use in smaller labs or clinics, providing ease of operation and quick results. Portable analysers are lightweight, battery-operated devices that allow for on-site testing, ensuring immediate access to coagulation data in various settings, including emergency responses.</p></p>
