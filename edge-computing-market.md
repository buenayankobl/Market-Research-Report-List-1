<p><p>Edge Computing refers to the practice of processing data near the source of data generation rather than relying solely on centralized data centers. This approach enables faster data processing and reduced latency, making it ideal for applications in IoT, autonomous vehicles, and smart cities. As more devices become interconnected, the demand for real-time data analysis and decision-making is driving the growth of edge computing solutions.</p><p>The Edge Computing Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 17.80% during the forecast period, reflecting a significant shift in how data is managed across industries. Notable trends include increased adoption of IoT devices, advancements in 5G technology, and the growing need for enhanced data security, as processing data closer to the source helps mitigate security risks. Additionally, sectors such as healthcare, manufacturing, and transportation are increasingly leveraging edge computing to optimize operations and improve efficiency. </p><p>As organizations look to harness the power of real-time insights, the edge computing market is poised for substantial expansion, impacting a wide range of applications and driving innovative solutions in various verticals.</p></p>
