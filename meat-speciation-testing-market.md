ELISA (Enzyme-Linked Immunosorbent Assay) is an immunological method that detects specific proteins, ensuring species verification through antibody interactions. The molecular diagnostics market includes advanced technologies like next-generation sequencing, facilitating comprehensive analysis of meat composition and enhancing regulatory compliance and consumer trust in food products.</p></p>
