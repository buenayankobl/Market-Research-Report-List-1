<p><p>The diffraction gratings elements market is primarily categorized into two types: holographic gratings and ruled gratings. Holographic gratings are created using interference patterns of laser light, offering high efficiency and precision, making them ideal for applications in spectroscopy and telecommunications. Ruled gratings, on the other hand, are produced by physically etching lines onto a surface, providing robust performance and broader wavelength range. Both types are crucial for controlling light in optical devices, enhancing their functionality across various industries.</p></p>
