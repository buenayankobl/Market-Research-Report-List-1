<p><p>The Aircraft Solid State Power Controller (SSPC) market is bifurcated into Single-Phase and Three-Phase Controllers, catering to different power management needs in aviation systems. In contrast, Three-Phase Controllers handle higher power loads, essential for larger aircraft and complex systems.
