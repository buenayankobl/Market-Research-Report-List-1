<p><ul><li>Inlay Type</li><li>Externally Assisted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Shoulder Orthosis System market can be divided into two main types: Inlay Type and Externally Assisted. Inlay Type orthoses are designed to fit directly within the shoulder joint, providing support and stability through a molded structure that aligns with the body's anatomy. Externally Assisted orthoses, on the other hand, utilize external straps and supports to aid movement and stabilize the shoulder without direct contact with the joint itself. Both types aim to enhance recovery, improve mobility, and prevent further injury.</p></p>
